About and Works

Personal

I am a twenty-year-old computer science and mathematics student pursuring my bachelor's at San Diego State University. As a third-year student with potentially extended study time, I seek to advance my professional development through concrete projects and research.

Work

I currently work for San Diego State as an Instructional Student Assistant, under Dr. Vadim Ponomarenko for his discrete mathematics course. My role is to guide students through the course, hosting weekly office hours. I review student progress and extend education beyond the lectures.

Previously, I worked as an after-school K12 instructor in JavaScript programming. While I worked with children to inspire knowledge in CS, I also managed the premises' computer network and navigated an educator relationship with students' parents.
